Creating the Routes
Firstly, we need to consider which routes our application will have. We’ll need
to perform all of the CRUD options we created in the back end.
• 	/words —display all the words in the database
• 	/words/new —create a new word
• 	/words/:id —display a word
• 	/words/:id/edit —edit a word
• 	/test —test your knowledge
• 	Deleting a word doesn’t require its own route. We’ll just fire off the
appropriate request and redirect to /words.
